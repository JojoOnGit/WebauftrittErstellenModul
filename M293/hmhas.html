Album Info

Hit Me Hard and Soft (2024)

Billie Eilish's third studio album was released on May 17, 2024, and was entirely written and produced by Billie and her brother Finneas O'Connell.

Unlike previous albums, Billie chose not to release any singles before the album's release, presenting it as a complete work of art meant to be experienced from beginning to end.

My Review

Billie's third album "Hit Me Hard and Soft" is an absolute revelation to me. From the first listen, I was completely immersed in the atmospheric soundscape that flows throughout the entire album. What impresses me most is the emotional maturity that Billie displays here - she's miles away from the teenager who released "When We All Fall Asleep".

My favorite songs are definitely "Lunch" with its pulsating beat and unexpected harmonic shifts, and "Birds of a Feather," which makes me sing along every single time. The way Finneas' production caresses Billie's voice is simply masterful.

What sets this album apart from her earlier work is its coherence. It truly feels like a connected journey where each song has its proper place. The decision not to release any singles in advance now makes perfect sense - this album needs to be experienced as a whole.

The introspective lyrics have moved me to tears multiple times, especially in "Bittersuite," where Billie reflects on past relationships. This song hits me right in the heart every time.

If I had to recommend one album from this year, it would definitely be "Hit Me Hard and Soft." It's musically innovative, emotionally profound, and showcases an artist at the height of her creative power.
